Magni 6 Midi


The Magni 6 Midi is a next-generation industrial-grade robotic platform designed for mapping larger areas, transporting heavier payloads, and automating tasks in factory or human-dangerous environments. Its robust design and modularity make it ideal for industrial applications, research, and advanced automation projects.

Variants

The Magni 6 Midi will be available in three variants to suit different operation needs:

  • 2-Wheel Drive (2WD): Efficient for ligther payloads and agile navigation.

  • 4-Wheel Drive (4WD): Balanced option for heavier payloads and rougher terrain.

  • 6-Wheel Drive (6WD): Maximum stability and load capacity for the most demanding industrial tasks.


Sensors

The Magni 6 Midi comes equipped with key modules to support industrial and research tasks:

  • Pi Camera 3 Module for high-resolution visual input.

  • Raspberry Pi Display for local monitoring and interface.

  • Large or Small LiDAR depending on customer needs for mapping or navigation,
